Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2024

1. Information We Collect

When you donate, sign up for our newsletter, or volunteer, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, and payment information (processed securely via Stripe/PayPal).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Process tax-deductible donations and issue receipts.
  • Send you updates about our sanctuary (if opted in).
  • Improve our website and donor experience.

3. Donor Privacy

We will never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to other organizations.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@stgertrudegardens.org.
