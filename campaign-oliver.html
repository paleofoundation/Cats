The Incident

Oliver is not a full-time resident, but a familiar face who visits our gardens daily for food and affection. Three nights ago, he arrived at our gates dragging his back legs. X-rays confirmed our worst fears: he had been shot. The bullet caused severe damage to his hip and spine, but our vets believe he has a fighting chance to walk again with specialized surgery.

His Spirit

Despite the pain, Oliver is incredibly sweet. He purrs when the nurses change his bandages and kneads his blankets with his front paws. He hasn't given up on humans, and we won't give up on him.
