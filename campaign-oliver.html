Remembering Oliver

Oliver, a beloved regular visitor to our sanctuary, survived a senseless act of cruelty and received emergency veterinary care through the Gardens. We later lost him under circumstances we believe involved human harm.

Retired Campaign In Memory

This page is preserved as a memorial and archive. Gifts made in Oliver's memory now support emergency veterinary care, protection, and prevention work for cats still at risk.

The Incident

Oliver was not a full-time resident, but a familiar face who visited our gardens daily for food and affection. He once arrived at our gates dragging his back legs. X-rays confirmed our worst fears: he had been shot. His case showed how quickly a community cat can become an emergency case.

What Oliver's story now funds

  • Emergency veterinary care through partners such as Limassol Veterinary Clinic.
  • Protection and recovery support for injured community cats.
  • Prevention work that reduces the number of cats left exposed to harm.

His Spirit

Despite the pain, Oliver was incredibly sweet. He purred when nurses changed his bandages and kneaded his blankets with his front paws. His story remains here because the work continues for cats like him.
