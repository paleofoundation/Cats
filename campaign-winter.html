Why We Need Your Help

Cyprus winters can be surprisingly harsh, especially for senior cats and those recovering from illness. Our current outdoor enclosure heaters are aging and inefficient, leaving gaps where the cold creeps in.

The Plan

  • Replace 15 outdated heating units in the main sanctuary pods.
  • Install industrial-grade insulation in the 3 newest enclosures.
  • Purchase 50 thermal heating pads for the senior ward.

Impact

Your donation directly warms a cat. A $50 donation provides a thermal pad that lasts for years. A $200 donation insulates an entire sleeping bay.
