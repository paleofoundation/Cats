A Sanctuary for the Future

This expansion is not just about land; it's about the future of cat rescue in Cyprus. Maneesh's Park will be a dedicated, secure environment designed from the ground up to offer the highest standard of care, freedom, and safety for our most vulnerable residents.

The Vision

  • 750 square meters of secure, open garden space.
  • Capacity to rescue and rehabilitate 200+ additional cats annually.
  • Dedicated zones for senior cats and those with special needs.
  • Beautiful, natural landscaping integrated with enrichment structures.
