Steward Access & Reporting

Stewards of the Garden receive direct visibility into the month they support. This includes a detailed financial summary showing how funds were applied across care categories, along with confirmation of expenses for food, veterinary services, and daily operations.

During the sponsored month, Stewards receive periodic visual updates from the sanctuary, including photographs and short video footage documenting daily life, medical care, and recovery. These updates are not curated marketing materials, but authentic records of care in progress.

Stewards are also provided with a month-end impact report summarizing the number of cats supported, veterinary interventions delivered, and stabilization outcomes achieved during the period they funded.

Access to behind-the-scenes updates and advance insight into upcoming needs or initiatives is included, allowing Stewards to understand operational realities and long-term planning at the sanctuary. All standard donation receipts and documentation are, of course, provided for record-keeping and tax purposes.
